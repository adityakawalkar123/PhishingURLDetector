What is Phishing?

Phishing attacks use deceptive URLs to impersonate trusted websites — tricking users into revealing passwords, credit cards, or personal data. Over 3.4 billion phishing emails are sent daily worldwide.

🛡️

How We Detect It

Our heuristic engine runs 6 security checks on every URL — analyzing protocol security, domain patterns, keyword traps, IP spoofing, character anomalies, and subdomain depth.

⚡

Instant Analysis

Results are returned in milliseconds. Each check contributes to a weighted risk score (0–100). The final verdict classifies the URL as Safe, Suspicious, or High Risk.
